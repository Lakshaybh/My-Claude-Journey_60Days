    About This Report

    This report reflects patterns noticed across three short exercises: scenario choices, a priority ranking, and two positioning timelines. Percentages describe tendencies, not fixed traits — most people carry a blend, and the same person can lean differently under different circumstances. This is an educational, self-reflection tool only, and is not a psychological, clinical, or diagnostic assessment.

        How This Report Was Built

        Each chapter contributed differently to your pattern scores. Chapter 1 scenarios weighted your chosen response directly toward the tendency it represents. Chapter 2 weighted your priority ranking by position — items ranked higher counted more heavily. Chapter 3 weighted your timeline placement by distance from the center — the further toward an edge, the stronger that pull; positions near the middle counted toward the Balanced Reflective Thinker tendency instead. All raw scores were then converted into the percentages shown in this report.

        Because this method blends several small, everyday-style scenarios rather than a single long test, treat the result as a snapshot of tendencies noticed today — not a permanent trait. Revisiting this exercise on a different day, in a different mood, may surface a different blend.
