About the Correspondent

I'm a curious, driven Data Science enthusiast currently pursuing my B.Tech at KCC Institute of Technology and Management (Graduating 2027). I love diving into messy real-world problems and turning raw data into insights that actually matter.

Whether it's building predictive models, designing clean dashboards, or exploring how AI fits into everyday life, I'm always pushing myself to learn, experiment, and stay ahead of the curve.

Over the years, I've built a solid foundation in Python, MySQL, Power BI, and emerging AI tools. Working with real datasets is genuinely my comfort zone — finding patterns, creating stories from numbers, and solving problems that connect tech with real-world impact.

The fast-changing AI ecosystem excites me deeply. I seek internships and hands-on project opportunities where data-driven solutions make a tangible difference for real people.
