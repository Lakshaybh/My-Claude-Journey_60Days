Key Research Observations & Anomalies
Arsenic & Fluoride Groundwater Anomaly: Patna

Groundwater analysis in Patna registers critical trace level heavy metals (specifically Arsenic) that are known to bypass domestic mesh filters, posing high long-term skin toxicity and inflammatory cell hazards compared to surface municipal reservoirs.

Low Mineral Desalination Paradox: Dubai

Dubai features a moderate annual AQI (92) due to desert sands. However, its desalinated water supply scores exceptionally low in hardness (95 mg/L), keeping scalp hydration levels and hair cuticle health high, despite lack of raw trace minerals.

Chalk Aquifer Hardness: London

London maintains a clean, low atmospheric pollution rating (AQI 45). However, municipal water is drawn predominantly from chalk aquifers, giving it a high water hardness rating (220 mg/L) that causes scalp dryness and scaling.
