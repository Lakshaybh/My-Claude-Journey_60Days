Healthcare Risk Control Tower

You are the Chief Medical Officer running a large hospital network. Patient-risk alerts keep coming in. Read each one, pick the response that fits, and hold your clinical KPIs up before the 3-minute shift ends.

  • Handle critical risks first. Ignored ones escalate and cost you patient safety.
  • Every response is a trade-off. The best one protects lives and lowers future risk.
  • Some shortcuts come back to hurt you a few seconds later, so watch the toasts.
  • It gets busier as the clock runs: more alerts, arriving faster, stacked together.

How to play

Manage clinical risk across your hospital network in real time.

  • Goal: keep patients safe and score as high as you can in 3 minutes.
  • Alerts arrive with a priority and a countdown. If a timer hits zero, the risk escalates and you take a penalty.
  • Responses each affect your KPIs differently. Matching the right response to the risk earns the most points.
  • KPIs: Patient Safety, Patient Satisfaction, Bed Capacity, Care Efficiency, Operating Cost, Lives Protected.
  • Delayed effects: shortcuts like "Ignore" may look free now but backfire later.
  • Grade: A+ to D based on your final score and KPI health.
