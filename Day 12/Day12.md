# Day 12 of 60 — Job Search Toolkit & Personal Branding

## Today's Focus

Modern hiring goes far beyond resumes. Recruiters evaluate communication, personal branding, outreach quality, positioning, and overall professional presence. Today we built a complete job search toolkit covering:

- **ATS Optimization** — Cover letter formatted for ATS parsing
- **Recruiter Outreach** — Emails structured to get responses
- **Personal Branding** — UVP, positioning statement, LinkedIn headline
- **Career Strategy** — Skill gap analysis with 3-month roadmap
